A Collection in Oppia is a library of lessons. Oppia has several collections, including the “Fractions” collection. Within the “Fractions” collections there are different explorations that help a student strengthen their fraction skills.

At Oppia, the lessons we provide for students are called explorations. All Explorations are comprised of images, videos, and questions that help students explore and strengthen their knowledge on various topics in a fun and interactive way. The first exploration in the Fraction collection is called “What is a Fraction”. It lays the necessary groundwork and introduces the topic to students first learning about fractions.
